About Us

The Department of Computer Science at Ramanujan College offers a dynamic and comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology. Our faculty members are experienced professionals dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

Academic Programs

We offer the following undergraduate programs:

  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

For more information on course structure and admission requirements, please visit our official website.

Facilities

Our department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to enhance the learning experience:

  • Modern Computer Labs with high-speed internet access
  • Access to the latest software and programming tools
  • Dedicated spaces for group discussions and projects
  • Regular workshops and seminars with industry experts
